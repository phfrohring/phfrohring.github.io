Why should I be paid?

Because I bring customers to the company.

  • Model
    1. A customer sees my marketing online.
      1. He sees the travel offer and a promo code: “send me back your invoice at this address and I will give you X$ through .”
    2. He buys a travel and send me back his invoice.
    3. I check with the seller the validity of the invoice.
    4. If the invoice is verified, I get paid Y% of the customer’s payment to the seller and I send X$ to the customer.
  • How can it go wrong?
    1. Someone can try to fake an invoice.
    2. Someone can flood my email and ransom me to stop it.
    3. The seller can pretend that a valid transaction is not valid.
  • Countermeasures:
    1. … fails because of Model.3
    2. … if attack not too sophisticated, can just block some emails else unfortunate inescapable circumstance.
    3. … select trusted sellers + low incentive: they are stealing me and from their customers they are supposed to spend time with.
